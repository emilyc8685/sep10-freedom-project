# Presentation Plan

## Hook
* I know many students who use their chromebook in class and instead of doing the classwork they would open another tab to play games. With my product I wanted to get rid of this problem and all other distractions technology brings. 

## Product
* This desk is built with a tablet into it and with the tablet students can access an app that's part of the product.
* The app makes it easy for teachers to assign work and this makes it easy for students to be engaged in the class work instead of getting distracted from other apps. 

## Process
*  I started by researching problems with education and technology and I found out that technology had some negative impact on students so I tried to make a product that could help with this problem
* By looking at educational softwares and hardwares I used them as ideas and I began to brainstorm ideas for my product
* I was able to create my website with the bootstrap grid system and bootstrap components as well as planning the layout out with my wireframe


## Conclusion
* The goal of my Smart Desk was to combine hardware and software to create a product to eliminate distractions in a classroom
