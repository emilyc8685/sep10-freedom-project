Hi! My name is Emily and I know many students who play games in class instead of doing their class work this is why I made a website and product to solve this problem. My solution was the Smart Desk. The Smart Desk is the combination of software and hardware since my product is a desk with a built in screen that comes with an app. The app makes it easy for teachers to assign work while students stay engaged in their classwork since they're unable to open other apps. This desk with solve the negative impacts that technology has on education. I was able to brainstorm ideas for my product by reasearching already existing softwares and hardwares and use them as inspiration for my own product. The tool I used for my website was SASS which gave CSS features it doesn't have. To create my website I used bootstrap components like a navbar and the bootstrap grid system to make my websute responsive. Thank you!
